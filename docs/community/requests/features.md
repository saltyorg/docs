# Features

We welcome suggestions for genuine enhancements to the Saltbox ecosystem. Please submit requests that add real value, not workarounds for issues already addressed or support questions.

## Guidelines

- Ensure your request is a genuine enhancement, not a workaround for a problem.
- If your idea is actually a solution to a support issue, please use our [Discord](https://discord.gg/ugfKXpFND8) for support instead.
- Check other requests to avoid duplicates.
- Be specific about the problem your feature solves.
- Suggest possible implementation details if you have them.

## Sandbox

You can suggest new apps for inclusion in our repositories by opening a request.

Be aware that developers are volunteers, and those who contribute new roles are generally expected to help maintain them. As a result, they likely won't explore unfamiliar apps just to fulfill a request, and the requester is often best suited to take on the role.

App requests also help gauge community interestâ€”roles with more support or "votes" are more likely to be picked up by contributors. If a request receives no activity, it will become stale and end up closed automatically, so showing your support by commenting or reacting can help keep it active.

[Submit a Sandbox app request](https://github.com/saltyorg/Sandbox/issues)

## Saltbox

We welcome enhancement ideas for the Saltbox repository.

App requests belong in the Sandbox repository and will be rejected if submitted here.

[Submit a Saltbox feature request](https://github.com/saltyorg/Saltbox/issues)

## `sb`

We welcome enhancement ideas relating to the Saltbox command-line tool.

[Submit an sb-go feature request](https://github.com/saltyorg/sb-go/issues)

## Docs

We welcome suggestions for improving our documentation.

[Submit a docs feature request](https://github.com/saltyorg/docs/issues)

## How to Submit

- Use the "Feature Request" template in the relevant repository.
- Clearly describe your proposed feature, its benefits, and why it is needed.
- Include relevant context, examples, or use cases.
