# Developing a Role

Interested in creating a new role for Sandbox? We welcome your contributions!

## Getting Started

- Review existing roles to understand structure and best practices.
- Fork the repository and create a new branch for your role.
- Develop your role following the established conventions.

## Guidelines

- Use clear, descriptive names for your role and tasks.
- Document your role with comments and usage instructions.
- Test your role thoroughly before submitting.

## Submitting Your Role

- Commit your changes with a meaningful message.
- Open a pull request describing your role and its purpose.
- The team will review and provide feedback.

## Need Help?

For questions or assistance, join our [Discord](https://discord.gg/ugfKXpFND8).
